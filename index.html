Tarot Cards

The Major Arcana is a 22 card set within the tarot that is considered to be the core and the foundation for the deck. All of the deck is filled with archetypal significance, but this is most pronounced within the Major Arcana. Rest of 78 cards are minor arcana represented by suits of
wands - fire, passion, inspiration,
cups - water, emotions, the unconscious,
swards - air, intelligence,
pentacles - earth, security, stability.
